# SweetBite Business Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the sales and inventory performance of SweetBite Chocolates using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to identify high-performing and low-performing products, evaluate inventory levels, and provide business recommendations for improving production planning.

## Business Problem
- Some chocolates sell out quickly while others remain in inventory.
- Manufacturing costs have increased.
- The company needs data-driven recommendations to optimize production.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Data Cleaning
- Business Analytics Reporting

## Key Findings
- Milk Chocolate recorded the highest sales.
- Dark Chocolate recorded the lowest sales.
- None of the products ran out of inventory during the observed period.
- Gift Box, Strawberry Delight, and Coffee Mocha had the highest manufacturing costs.

- For detailed business recommendations and supporting analysis, see the Business Report included in this repository.
